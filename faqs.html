What is counselling?
+

Counselling is short-term support to help you deal with specific problems or current challenges. It focuses on finding solutions, building coping skills, and helping you feel more in control.

Example: If you're struggling after the loss of a loved one, counselling can help you process grief, manage emotions, and cope with daily life.

4
What is therapy?
+

Therapy is longer-term support that helps you understand deeper issues and patterns in your life. It focuses on healing, personal growth, and lasting change.

Example: If you're dealing with the effects of childhood trauma, therapy can help you heal emotional pain and rebuild your sense of self.
