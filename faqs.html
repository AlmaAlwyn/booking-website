Frequently Asked Questions

What is Internal Family Systems (IFS)?

IFS is an evidence-based therapeutic approach that views the mind as composed of various "parts." It helps you understand and harmonize these parts to achieve inner balance and healing.

What is a rapport session?

A rapport session is a free one-hour introductory meeting for new users to discuss your needs, explore the IFS approach, and determine if we’re a good fit to work together.

How long are therapy sessions?

Standard therapy sessions are 50 minutes long, conducted both virtually and in-person. Extended sessions may be available upon request.

How do I schedule a session?

You can schedule a session by visiting the Schedule a Session page and filling out the form. I’ll respond via WhatsApp within 24 hours with "Accept" or "Reject."

What are your fees?

Fees vary based on session type and duration. Rapport sessions are free for new users. Please contact me via WhatsApp for detailed pricing information.
