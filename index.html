About the Work

Offering Compassionate Support for Your Healing Journey

I offer you a welcoming safe space where you can show up just as you are. Where your anger, shame, sadness, and resistance are all welcome and held. My intention is to help you in healing from the inside out and reconnect with your inner strength and wisdom. Together we can heal emotional wounds, resolve internal conflicts, and move through feeling stuck in life.

Take the first step toward change.

Sessions are available both in person and online, so you can choose what feels right whether you’re nearby or across the globe.

What Sets My Therapy Apart

Offerings to Support Your Growth

Whether you’re new to IFS, on a healing journey, or a practitioner seeking growth, I offer a supportive space to learn, connect, and transform.

IFS Therapy

One-on-one IFS sessions to help you explore, understand, and heal your inner parts with compassion, curiosity, and deep connection to your true Self.

IFS Groups

Connect with others in a safe, supportive space. Group sessions offer shared insight, reflection, and healing through the power of the IFS model.

Slowdown Sundays Circle

Slowdown Sundays Circle is a weekly in-person gathering created to support emotional and mental well-being, held every Sunday evening in Central Delhi.
