Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Abundance Being, I am committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how I collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit my website or engage with my services.

I may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide when scheduling a session or contacting me via WhatsApp. I do not collect sensitive payment information, as payments are handled securely through third-party platforms.

How I Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiries via email or WhatsApp.
  • Improve my services and website functionality.

Data Security

I take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

I use WhatsApp to communicate with clients. Please review WhatsApp's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information. Contact me to exercise these rights.
