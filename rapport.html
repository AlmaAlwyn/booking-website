What is a Consultation?

A consultation is simply a comfortable conversation where we get to know each other. In this 60-minute talk, I'll listen to your concerns and explain how we can work together. Your initial consultation is complimentary.

What we will do:

  • Talk about what's bothering you - Share your concerns in a safe space
  • Learn how therapy can help - Simple, clear information
  • Ask all your questions - No question is too small
  • Decide if we're a good fit - Make sure you feel comfortable

What you will experience:

  • Complete privacy and confidentiality
  • No judgement, just understanding
  • No pressure to continue after the consultation
  • No cost for your initial consultation
